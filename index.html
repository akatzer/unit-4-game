Infinity Stone Collector Game

Hello, Tony Stark!

Thanos is collecting the Infinity Stones and you need to beat him to the punch!

Thanos will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are six Infinity Stones to the right. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You beat Thanos by matching your total score to his number and you lose the game if your total score goes over his.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it and the values change every game.
